## Skipped — NO UNIVERSAL DEFAULT

- **CLADDING (above ground)** — Building Safety Act / Approved Document B fire requirements diverge for buildings above 11m vs above 18m vs below 11m. A2-s1,d0 / non-combustible required >11m on residential. No single default works across the catalogue's 10 codes. Source: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/external-wall-systems-on-buildings
- **GAS HOB / GAS COOKER** — Brand-new gas hobs in flats must include Flame Supervision Device per Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations 1998; otherwise must conform to BS 5386-3 / EN 30. Replacement specifics depend on flue/ventilation arrangement of the dwelling. Source: https://www.gassaferegister.co.uk/gas-safety/gas-appliances/gas-cooker-and-hob-appliance-guide/
- **STAIRLIFT** — Specification is occupant-specific (curve/straight, weight, mobility profile, DFG-funded vs private). No standard product. Source: https://www.gov.uk/disabled-facilities-grants
- **PV SOLAR INSTALLATION** — Sizing is roof/orientation/load specific; MCS-certified install required for grants but kit composition varies. Source: https://mcscertified.com/
- **BUILDING/MAST WIND TURBINES** — Negligible deployment in social housing; planning-and-noise constrained. No convention.
- **WASTE CHUTE** — Use class is high-rise specific; not a domestic default.
- **STAIR LIFT / GROUND SOURCE HEAT PUMPS** — Site-specific (borehole/loop area); MCS certified but kit varies.
