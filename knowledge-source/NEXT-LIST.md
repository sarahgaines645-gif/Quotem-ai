## RICS — Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors

### Goal
Get QB2 to chartered-surveyor-fluent — knows the standards, the report formats, the professional duties.

### Sources to gather
- **RICS Home Survey Standard 2024** (current edition) — the mandatory standard for residential surveying — https://www.rics.org/profession-standards/rics-standards-and-guidance
- **RICS Home Survey Levels 1, 2, 3** — what each level covers (Condition Report / Homebuyer Report / Building Survey)
- **RICS Valuation — Global Standards (Red Book)** — for any valuation-touching work
- **RICS Code of Conduct (2022 review)**
- **Defects Reporting Guide** — common defects + how to describe them
- **Damp Investigation Guide** (RICS)

### Format
Most are members-only PDFs. If Sarah has RICS membership (or a friendly chartered surveyor user) we can grab them. Otherwise focus on the Home Survey Standard which has free public summaries.

📂 **Save as:** `tier4-rics-home-survey-standard-FULL.txt` and similar

## Decent Homes Standard

### Goal
Now that **Renters' Rights Act 2025 Part 3** extends DHS to private rented sector, QB2 should know what it actually requires.

### Sources to gather
- **Original Decent Homes Standard** (DCLG 2006) — gov.uk
- **DLUHC consultation on extending DHS to PRS** (response published 2024)
- **The four DHS criteria:**
  1. Statutory minimum standard for housing (no Cat 1 HHSRS hazards)
  2. Reasonable state of repair
  3. Reasonably modern facilities and services
  4. Reasonable degree of thermal comfort

📂 **Save as:** `tier4-decent-homes-standard-FULL.txt`
